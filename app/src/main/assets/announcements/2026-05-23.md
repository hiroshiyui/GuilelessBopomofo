# 2026.05.23 公告 Announcement

樸實注音鍵盤的起始動機，是我為了想要有自己的一套「可用許氏鍵盤」、「美觀」、「安全、完全離線」、「求職能力展示」的輸入法編輯器專案。它絕大多數時候 (99.99999%) 是個一人專案，背後並沒有商業資源挹注，那些在 Google Play 評論批評「你們」的，拜託一下好嗎？根本沒有什麼「你們」，就只有「我」，只有我一人而已，好唄？

The original motivation for Guileless Bopomofo was to create an IME (Input Method Editor) project that supports "Hsu's Layout," is "aesthetic," "secure/fully offline," and serves as a "portfolio for job hunting." For the vast majority of the time (99.99999%), it has been a one-person project without any commercial backing. To those criticizing "you guys" in Google Play reviews: please, give me a break. There is no "you guys"—it's just "me." Only me. Okay?

在今年四月，我原本為了方便接案、能守法開發票而設的一人公司，因為長期以來入不敷出，已經解散倒閉了。算算我投入在樸實注音鍵盤的時間與精力，得到的回報，除一下，啊！趨近於零！蒸蚌！

This April, the one-person company I set up for freelancing and legal invoicing was dissolved due to long-term financial losses. If I calculate the return on the time and energy I've invested in Guileless Bopomofo... ah, it's approaching zero! Splendid!

別人拿著我做的軟體得到了生產力，我得到的是零工作機會，偶爾還要被花式三星好評與 GitHub issue 說我做的還不夠好。

While others gain productivity from the software I built, I get zero job opportunities. Occasionally, I even get "creative" 3-star reviews or GitHub issues telling me that my work isn't good enough.

可不可以拜託一下？可不可以有點分寸？可不可以饒了我？我這樣做的還不夠嗎？

Could I just ask for a bit of consideration? Could you please give me a break? Haven't I done enough already?
