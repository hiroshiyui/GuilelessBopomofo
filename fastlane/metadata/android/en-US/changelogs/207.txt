Hi there! Guileless Bopomofo 4.0.7 is here with a small but handy improvement for people who switch between the on-screen keyboard and a hardware keyboard.

* Added a separate conversion engine preference for when a physical keyboard is attached. Pick your favorite engine for the on-screen keyboard in General settings, and a different one for the hardware keyboard in Physical Keyboard settings. The IME switches automatically.

Thanks for using Guileless Bopomofo!
